System Conception & Domain Architecture

Bengaluru's massive geographic expansion creates intricate transit challenges. This open platform bypasses standard administrative delays by giving community members direct visibility over public streets. By contrasting the inherent structural beauty of the city with the everyday bottlenecks faced by commuters, GridlockPulse provides a direct, user-vouched data network.

Future updates will introduce live geofenced map links, allowing drivers to pinpoint specific road hazards, vote on delay accuracy, and calculate alternative, dynamic paths around high-volume jams smoothly.
