Project Domain Reflection & Target Outcomes

Bengaluru's vehicular volumes frequently saturate key arteries, transforming daily commutes into multi-hour endurance tasks. The objective of our MERN Full-Stack Prototype is to leverage the telemetry of decentralized, crowdsourced commuter logs to deliver accurate spatial insights[cite: 259, 261].

By evaluating inputs against historical baselines, this smart-city initiative addresses structural patterns. It isolates high-incidence corridors, optimizes institutional resource deployment, and provides structural, data-backed insights directly to local transit authorities[cite: 259, 261].
