Domain Framework & System Vision

Bengaluru's immense geographic expansion presents deep transit management problems. Rapid development combined with surging commuter dependencies often triggers vast logistical bottlenecks. The overarching goal of our MERN Full-Stack Prototype architecture is to create a crowdsourced network ecosystem that changes how real-time bottlenecks are handled.

By building this clean frontend architecture, we establish an intuitive structural bridge. Future database collections will process these verified, localized incidents to output unified analysis dashboards. This lets transit administrators instantly isolate high-congestion vectors and accurately prioritize operational traffic details.
